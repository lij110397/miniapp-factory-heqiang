Factory Introduction

Company Profile

Heqiang Leather was established in 2005 and is a modern enterprise specializing in the research, development, production and sales of leather shoe materials. The company is located in Dongguan City, Guangdong Province, covering an area of 5,000 square meters, with modern production workshops and advanced production equipment.

We are committed to providing high-quality leather materials for global footwear manufacturers. Our products are exported to Europe, America, Southeast Asia and other regions, and we have established long-term stable cooperative relationships with many internationally renowned brands.
